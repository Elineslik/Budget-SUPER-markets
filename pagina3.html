Tips for grocery shopping

Feeding yourself can be very expensive. Especially when you are surrounded by a
city full of delicious takeaway places and plenty of home delivery services. Being
a student, you most likely live on a
budget. Therefore it is important you
also find cheap ways to fill your
stomach.

Do groceries for the whole week, because you will be more conscious of what you're buying.

Buy products in larger packages, as this also saves you money.

Keep an eye on the offers. Supermarkets produce weekly leaflets with their offers. You can find them at reclamefolder.nl.

Buy products from the lower shelves. These are cheaper

Buy your fruit and vegetables at the market.

Only go shopping with a full stomach. This will prevent you from buying more than you need.

If you shop at Albert Heijn (AH), don’t forget your bonuscard for discounts.

Don’t shop at AH To Go or smaller supermarkets at stations. Groceries are more expensive there.
