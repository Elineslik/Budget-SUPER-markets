Ten Katemarkt

Ten Katestraat 34, 1053 CG Amsterdam

If you are looking for a convenient market to visit, this is it. This market is located on the Kinkerstraat which you can easily reach by public transport. It offers excellent value fresh fruit and vegetables – along with flowers, cheese, world foods, street snacks, and so on.

Lex Groente

Lex Groente is the best and cheapest fruit and vegetable stall of the Ten Katemarkt. This is therefore the best place for students on a budget to buy fruit and vegetables.

You can also have the cheapest fruit and vegetables delivered. What is of course ideal for busy students.

Here's a list of a few fruits and vegetables that lex Groente sells.

  • 3 red peppers for €1
  • 500 grams broccoli for €2
  • Lettuce for €0,75
  • 500 grams onions for €1
  • One avocado for €0,75
  • A mango for €0,75
